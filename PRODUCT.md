# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
- **Superadmins**: The company managing clients, services, and hostel tutors.
- **Admins & Managers**: Managing daily hostel and mess operations, billing, and student records.
- **Students**: Accessing menus, checking bills, and interacting with hostel services.

## Product Purpose
A multi-tier management system for hostels and messes. It connects the service provider company with individual hostel administrators and their students. Success looks like streamlined daily operations, transparent billing, and effortless management across all tiers.

## Positioning
A comprehensive, all-in-one system for hostel and mess administration.

## Brand Personality
Modern, accessible, trustworthy.

## Anti-references
- No overly playful or cartoonish designs.
- Avoid clunky, outdated, or overly dense enterprise patterns.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness**: Keep workflows straightforward and data easy to read for daily operations.
- **Trust through professionalism**: The interface must inspire confidence, especially regarding billing and management.
- **Accessible to all roles**: The system must feel intuitive whether the user is a superadmin, a daily manager, or a student.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility. Ensure keyboard navigation and maintain good contrast (WCAG standards) throughout the application.
